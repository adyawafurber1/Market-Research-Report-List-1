<p><strong>The Lead Forming Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Lead Forming Machine</li><li>Radial Lead Forming Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lead forming machine market comprises two primary types: axial lead forming machines and radial lead forming machines. Axial lead forming machines bend wire leads parallel to the component's axis, ideal for axial components like resistors and capacitors. In contrast, radial lead forming machines arrange leads perpendicular to the body, accommodating radial components such as diodes and transistors. Both types enhance production efficiency and precision in electronic manufacturing, catering to the growing demand for compact and reliable electronic devices.</p></p>
