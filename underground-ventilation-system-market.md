<p><strong>The Underground Ventilation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Flow Fans System</li><li>Centrifugal Fans System</li><li>Mixed Flow Fans System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underground ventilation system market consists of various fan types designed to ensure proper airflow in subterranean environments. Axial flow fans move air parallel to the fan shaft, ideal for low-pressure, high-flow applications. Centrifugal fans, on the other hand, draw air at right angles to the flow, generating higher pressure suitable for long duct systems. Mixed flow fans combine features of both, providing versatile airflow solutions. Each type plays a crucial role in maintaining air quality and safety in underground operations.</p></p>
