<p><ul><li>Low Pass Filter</li><li>High Pass Filter</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LC filters market comprises various types, primarily categorized into low pass filters, high pass filters, and others. Low pass filters allow signals below a certain frequency to pass while attenuating higher frequencies, commonly used in audio and radio applications. High pass filters, on the other hand, permit signals above a specific frequency, useful in eliminating low-frequency noise. Other types include band-pass and band-stop filters, addressing specific frequency ranges for specialized applications like telecommunications and signal processing.</p></p>
