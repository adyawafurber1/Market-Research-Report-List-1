<p><ul><li>Asynchronous Type</li><li>Synchronous Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sample rate converters market is divided into two primary types: asynchronous and synchronous converters. Asynchronous type converters handle sample rate changes independently of the system clock, allowing flexibility in converting between varying sample rates without a common timing reference. In contrast, synchronous converters operate in harmony with the system clock, ensuring consistent timing and precision during conversion. Both types serve essential roles in audio processing, digital signal processing, and telecommunications, catering to different application needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
