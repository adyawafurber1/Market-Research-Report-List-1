<p><p>Fixed Neutral Density (ND) filters are essential tools in photography and videography, used to reduce the intensity of light without affecting color balance. The ND2 filter halves the light, allowing for longer exposures or wider apertures. ND4 reduces light to a quarter, ideal for capturing motion blur or shallow depth of field in brighter conditions. ND8 lowers light to one-eighth, suitable for creative effects or varying exposure times. Other types may include variable density filters and specialized designs for unique lighting situations.</p></p>
