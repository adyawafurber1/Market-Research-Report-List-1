<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Organ Transplantation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Autograft</li><li>Allograft</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organ transplantation market is categorized into three main types: autografts, allografts, and others. Autografts involve transplantation of organs or tissues from one part of a person's body to another, ensuring no risk of rejection. Allografts refer to transplants between individuals of the same species, usually necessitating immunosuppressive therapy to prevent rejection. The "others" category encompasses xenografts (from different species) and synthetic grafts, which are increasingly utilized as alternative solutions in transplantation processes.</p></p>
