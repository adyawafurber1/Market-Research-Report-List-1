<p><p>The home battery storage system market primarily features two types: lead-acid batteries and lithium-ion (Li-ion) batteries. Lead-acid batteries are cost-effective and widely used, offering reliable performance for off-grid applications, but they have a shorter lifespan and lower energy density. In contrast, lithium-ion batteries are more efficient, with higher energy density and longer lifespans, making them suitable for modern energy storage solutions. Their lightweight design and faster charge cycles contribute to their growing popularity in residential energy systems.</p></p>
