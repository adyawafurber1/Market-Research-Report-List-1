<p><strong>The Direct Burial Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid</li><li>Twisted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Direct Burial Cable market primarily consists of two types: solid and twisted cables. Solid cables feature a single, thick conductor, offering durability and effective transmission for short distances, making them suitable for local area connections. Twisted cables, on the other hand, consist of multiple copper wires twisted together, reducing electromagnetic interference and enhancing signal integrity over longer distances. Both types are designed for underground installations, providing protection against moisture and environmental factors, ensuring reliable communication and power transmission.</p></p>
