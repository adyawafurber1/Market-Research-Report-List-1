<p><ul><li>Temporary Hair Dye</li><li>Semi-permanent Hair Dye</li><li>Permanent Hair Dye</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hair color and dye market consists of three primary types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent hair dyes. Temporary hair dyes provide short-term color, typically lasting a few washes, making them ideal for experimentation or special occasions. Semi-permanent dyes last longer, usually up to several weeks, and gradually fade with washing. Permanent hair dyes penetrate the hair shaft for long-lasting color and require chemical processing, offering complete gray coverage and a wide range of vibrant shades. Each type caters to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
