<p><p>Water-based packaging adhesives are eco-friendly adhesives used in the packaging industry, primarily composed of water as a solvent. These adhesives are ideal for applications in food packaging, labeling, and flexible packaging, offering strong adhesion, durability, and safety.</p><p>The Water Based Packaging Adhesives Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.3% during the forecast period. Additionally, the shift toward recyclable and biodegradable packaging materials is boosting the adoption of water-based adhesives.
