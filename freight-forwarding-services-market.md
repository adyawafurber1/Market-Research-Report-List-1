<p><p>Freight forwarding services encompass various market types, including Full Container Load (FCL), Less-than Container Load (LCL), and others. FCL involves shipping a full container dedicated to a single shipment, making it cost-effective for large volumes. LCL, on the other hand, consolidates multiple shipments into one container, ideal for smaller loads that do not fill an entire container, thereby reducing costs for businesses. Other market options may include specialized logistics services or multimodal transport solutions to suit diverse shipping needs.</p></p>
