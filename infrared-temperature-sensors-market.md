<p><strong>The Infrared Temperature Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared temperature sensors market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact sensors. Contact type sensors measure temperature by physically touching the object, typically using thermocouples or thermistors, leading to precise readings but limited to stationary objects. In contrast, non-contact type sensors detect infrared radiation emitted by an object, allowing for temperature measurement from a distance, making them ideal for moving objects and hazardous environments. This distinction caters to diverse industry applications, enhancing temperature monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
