<p><p>The gravity casting machines market comprises two primary types: tilting and fixed gravity casting machines. Tilting gravity casting machines allow for the mold to be tilted during the pouring process, ensuring better material flow and reducing defects in complex shapes. In contrast, fixed gravity casting machines maintain a stationary position, relying on gravity to fill the mold, making them suitable for simpler designs and high-volume production. Each type serves distinct applications in manufacturing, impacting efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
