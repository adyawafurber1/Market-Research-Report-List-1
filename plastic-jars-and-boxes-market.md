<p><p>The plastic jars and boxes market is categorized based on the type of plastic used, including PET, PP, HDPE, and LDPE. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) is known for its strength and clarity, making it ideal for food packaging. PP (Polypropylene) offers flexibility and resistance to chemicals, commonly used for storage containers. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is durable and UV-resistant, suitable for outdoor applications. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is lightweight and flexible, often utilized for squeeze bottles and bags, enhancing diverse packaging solutions.</p></p>
