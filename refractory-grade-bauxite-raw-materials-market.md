<p><ul><li>Shaft Furnace Calcining Bauxite</li><li>Converter Calcining Bauxite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refractory grade bauxite raw materials market is primarily divided into two types based on the calcining process: shaft furnace calcining bauxite and converter calcining bauxite. Shaft furnace calcining bauxite is produced in vertical shaft furnaces, providing enhanced control over temperature and quality. In contrast, converter calcining bauxite is processed in rotary kilns or converters, offering more uniform heating and efficiency. Each type serves specialized applications in the refractory industry, meeting varying demands for strength and thermal stability.</p></p>
