<p><p>The bakery enzymes market includes various types such as amylase, glucose oxidase, lipase, lipoxygenase, xylanase, protease, and asparaginase. Amylase enhances dough stability and crust quality, while glucose oxidase improves bread texture and extends shelf life. Lipase contributes to the flavor and dough extensibility, and lipoxygenase aids in dough strengthening. Xylanase breaks down hemicellulose for better dough handling, protease aids in protein breakdown, and asparaginase reduces acrylamide formation during baking, catering to health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
