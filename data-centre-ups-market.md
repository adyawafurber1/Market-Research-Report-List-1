<p><strong>The Data Centre UPS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Battery-Operated</li><li>Flywheel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Data Centre UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) market primarily features two types: battery-operated systems and flywheel systems. Battery-operated UPS units utilize rechargeable batteries to provide backup power during outages, offering scalability and reliability for critical applications. In contrast, flywheel UPS systems store kinetic energy in a rotating mass, delivering short-term power backup with rapid response times. Both types serve crucial roles in ensuring data centre resilience and uptime, catering to varied energy storage and operational needs.</p></p>
