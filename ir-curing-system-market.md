<p><p>The IR curing system market is categorized into three primary types based on wavelength: shortwave, mediumwave, and longwave. Shortwave systems use high-intensity infrared radiation, offering rapid heating and curing, ideal for quick-drying applications. Mediumwave systems provide a balanced approach, delivering moderate heat for a range of materials, while longwave systems emit lower energy, suited for gentle curing processes, often in thicker substrates. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, enhancing efficiency and effectiveness in various curing applications.</p></p>
