<p><p>The DC current sensors market features two primary types: closed-loop and open-loop current sensors. Closed-loop sensors utilize feedback mechanisms to measure current, offering high accuracy and stability, making them suitable for precision applications. In contrast, open-loop sensors operate without feedback and are typically simpler and more cost-effective but lack the same level of accuracy. Both types are essential in various applications, including industrial automation, electric vehicles, and renewable energy systems, catering to different performance and budget needs.</p></p>
