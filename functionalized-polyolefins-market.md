<p><p>The functionalized polyolefins market includes various types based on the modification method. Randomly functionalized polyolefins have functional groups distributed throughout the polymer chain, allowing for diverse applications. End-functionalized polyolefins feature functional groups at the polymer's ends, enhancing reactivity for specific applications. Block functionalized polyolefins consist of distinct polymer segments with varying properties, providing tailored performance. Graft functionalized polyolefins have side chains attached to the main polymer backbone, offering unique characteristics that improve compatibility and performance in various formulations.</p></p>
