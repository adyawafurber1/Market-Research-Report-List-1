<p><p>The traction steel rope market encompasses various types used in elevator systems and lifting applications. Hoist ropes are crucial for raising and lowering elevators, while drum and counterweight ropes connect the hoist mechanism to counterweights, ensuring smooth operation. Compensating ropes maintain the system's balance and efficiency by offsetting weight changes, and governor ropes ensure safety by controlling the speed and position of the elevator car. Each type plays a vital role in the overall functionality and safety of vertical transportation systems.</p></p>
