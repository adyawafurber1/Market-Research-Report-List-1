<p><p>The aluminum roof coatings market is divided into two main types: fiber and non-fiber. Fiber-based coatings incorporate reinforcement materials to enhance durability and improve structural integrity, making them suitable for high-wear applications. Non-fiber coatings, on the other hand, focus on providing a reflective surface without added reinforcement, offering cost-effectiveness and ease of application. Both types serve the primary purpose of protecting roofs, improving energy efficiency, and extending the lifespan of roofing materials while reflecting solar heat.</p></p>
