<p><ul><li>Protein Concentrate</li><li>Protein Isolate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The textured protein market primarily comprises two types: protein concentrate and protein isolate. Protein concentrate is produced by removing a portion of non-protein components from plant sources, resulting in a product that contains around 60-80% protein. In contrast, protein isolate is more refined, with protein content typically exceeding 90%, achieved through extensive filtration processes. Both types are popular in food products, offering plant-based alternatives for meat and dairy, catering to the growing demand for healthier and sustainable protein options.</p></p>
