<p><ul><li>Single Pendulum Systems</li><li>Dual Pendulum Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rail windscreen wiper system market primarily consists of two types: single pendulum systems and dual pendulum systems. Single pendulum systems feature a single arm that pivots to clear the windscreen, typically offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, dual pendulum systems utilize two arms to enhance coverage and efficiency, providing a more thorough wipe across larger windscreen areas. Both systems are essential for ensuring visibility and safety for train operators under various weather conditions.</p></p>
