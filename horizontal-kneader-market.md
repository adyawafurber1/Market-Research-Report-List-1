<p><strong>The Horizontal Kneader Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dynamic</li><li>Static</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Horizontal Kneader market can be categorized into two main types: dynamic and static. Dynamic kneaders feature rotating blades that vigorously mix materials, ensuring consistent blending and enhanced processing efficiency. They are ideal for applications requiring thorough mixing of viscous or paste-like substances. In contrast, static kneaders utilize fixed blades, relying on material movement for mixing. This type offers lower energy consumption and is suitable for processes where shear levels need to be minimized. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
