<p><strong>The Ultrasound Units Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Ultrasound Units</li><li>Internal Ultrasound Units</li><li>Endoscopic Ultrasound Units</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasound units market comprises three main types: external, internal, and endoscopic ultrasound units. External ultrasound units are non-invasive devices used for diagnostic imaging by placing the transducer on the skin surface. Internal ultrasound units, also known as transvaginal or transrectal systems, involve the insertion of the ultrasound probe into the body to obtain clearer images of internal organs. Endoscopic ultrasound units combine endoscopy and ultrasonography, allowing detailed visualization of internal structures, particularly in gastrointestinal applications. Each type serves distinct diagnostic and therapeutic purposes.</p></p>
