<p><p>The mobile air purifier market comprises several types, each utilizing distinct technologies. HEPA filters effectively capture particulate matter, while activated carbon filters absorb odors and volatile organic compounds. Static electricity purifiers employ charged particles to remove pollutants. Ion purifiers release ions to eliminate contaminants, enhancing air quality. Ozone generators produce ozone to neutralize odors and pathogens, though safety concerns exist. Other types may incorporate advanced technologies like UV light or photocatalytic oxidation. Each type serves specific air quality needs.</p></p>
