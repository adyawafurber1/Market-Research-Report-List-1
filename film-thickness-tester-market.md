<p><p>The Film Thickness Tester market is primarily categorized into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I testers are typically designed for non-destructive measurements, often using technologies like optical or laser methods to assess film thickness without damaging the substrate. Type II testers, on the other hand, may involve more invasive techniques, such as mechanical or nuclear gauges, which can provide accurate measurements but may alter the sample. Each type serves distinct applications across industries, catering to specific measurement needs.</p></p>
