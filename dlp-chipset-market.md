<p><strong>The DLP Chipset Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DMD</li><li>DMD Controller</li><li>DMD Micromirror Drivers</li><li>Evaluation Board</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DLP chipset market encompasses various components, each serving distinct functions. DMD (Digital Micromirror Device) is the core component, utilizing thousands of micromirrors to modulate light. The DMD Controller manages the operation of the DMD, ensuring precise timing and image processing. DMD Micromirror Drivers control the individual micromirrors, facilitating dynamic image creation. Additionally, Evaluation Boards provide a platform for testing and developing DLP applications, allowing designers to prototype and optimize their systems effectively. These components collectively drive advancements in visual display technology.</p></p>
