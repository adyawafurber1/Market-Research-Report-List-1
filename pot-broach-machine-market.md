<p><strong>The Pot Broach Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight Pull</li><li>Spiral Pull</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pot broach machine market is segmented into two main types: straight pull and spiral pull. Straight pull broaching involves a linear motion where the broach is pulled straight through the workpiece, ideal for producing flat surfaces and keyways. In contrast, spiral pull broaching features a helical path, allowing for more complex shapes and deeper cuts, making it suitable for intricate designs. Each type serves distinct machining needs, impacting efficiency, precision, and production processes across various industries.</p></p>
