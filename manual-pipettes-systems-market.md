<p><p>The manual pipette systems market comprises primarily two types: single-channel pipettes and multichannel pipettes. Single-channel pipettes are designed for precise liquid dispensing, ideal for applications requiring accuracy with individual samples. In contrast, multichannel pipettes can dispense liquids across multiple channels simultaneously, enhancing efficiency in high-throughput settings, such as labs conducting numerous tests at once. Both types are essential in research, clinical, and quality control laboratories, offering flexibility and precision for various liquid handling tasks.</p></p>
