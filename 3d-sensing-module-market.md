<p><ul><li>TOF Technology 3D Sensing</li><li>Structured Light 3D Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D sensing module market comprises two primary technologies: Time-of-Flight (TOF) and Structured Light. TOF technology measures the time it takes for light to travel to an object and back, enabling precise depth perception and distance measurement, ideal for applications like facial recognition and gesture control. Structured Light uses projected patterns to analyze geometric shapes, creating a 3D image through distortion analysis, commonly utilized in augmented reality and object scanning. Both technologies enhance user interaction and functionality in various devices.</p></p>
