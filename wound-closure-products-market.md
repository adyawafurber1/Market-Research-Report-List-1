Surgical staples offer a quick and efficient method to close wounds, especially in surgery. Wound closure strips are adhesive strips used for superficial wounds, providing support while allowing flexibility. Adhesives and tissue sealants create a bond for wound edges, enhancing healing without the need for sutures. Hemostats are used to control bleeding during surgery and facilitate wound closure.</p></p>
