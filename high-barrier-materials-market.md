<p><p>High barrier materials are crucial in packaging to enhance shelf life and protect products from moisture, oxygen, and UV light. Ethylene vinyl alcohol (EVOH) excels in oxygen barrier protection, making it ideal for perishable goods. Polyethylene naphthalate (PEN) combines good barrier properties with higher thermal stability, suitable for applications in electronics and sensitive materials. Collectively, these materials are vital for maintaining product integrity and quality.</p></p>
