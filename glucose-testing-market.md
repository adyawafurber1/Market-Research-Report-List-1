In contrast, non-invasive testing aims to measure glucose levels without any skin penetration, utilizing technologies like infrared spectroscopy or bioelectric impedance. Both types cater to the growing demand for convenient and accurate glucose monitoring solutions for diabetes management and overall health.</p></p>
