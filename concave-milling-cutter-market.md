<p><p>The concave milling cutter market is segmented based on the number of teeth: below 10 teeth, 10-20 teeth, and above 20 teeth. Cutters with fewer than 10 teeth are typically used for rapid material removal in roughing operations, offering high chip clearance. Those with 10-20 teeth provide a balance between speed and precision, suitable for general machining tasks. Cutters with more than 20 teeth are ideal for finishing operations, delivering smoother surfaces and reduced tool marks while operating at slower feed rates.</p></p>
