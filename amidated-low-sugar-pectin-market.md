<p><strong>The Amidated Low Sugar Pectin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin</li><li>Low Methoxyl (LM) Pectin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Amidated low sugar pectin is categorized into two main types: High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin and Low Methoxyl (LM) Pectin. HM pectin typically requires high sugar content and acidity for gel formation, making it suitable for traditional jams and jellies. In contrast, LM pectin can gel with lower sugar levels and is often used in reduced-sugar or low-calorie products, as well as for products that need to maintain clarity and stability without high sugar concentrations. Both types cater to diverse food industry needs.</p></p>
