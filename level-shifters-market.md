<p><p>The level shifters market is categorized into unidirectional and bidirectional converters. Unidirectional level shifters allow signal transmission in one direction, making them ideal for applications requiring simple data flow, like interfacing low-voltage components with higher voltage circuits. In contrast, bidirectional converters facilitate data communication in both directions, catering to more complex applications, such as multi-voltage systems in microcontrollers or memory devices. These market segments address varying needs in electronics, enhancing device compatibility and functionality.</p></p>
