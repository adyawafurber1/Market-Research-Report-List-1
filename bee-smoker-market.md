<p><p>The bee smoker market can be categorized into two main types: manual bee smokers and electric bee smokers. Manual bee smokers operate using combustible materials like wood pellets or paper, producing smoke manually to calm bees during hive inspections. They are traditional and popular among hobbyist beekeepers. In contrast, electric bee smokers utilize battery power for consistent smoke production, offering convenience and ease of use. Both types serve the essential function of easing interactions with bees while ensuring hive safety.</p></p>
