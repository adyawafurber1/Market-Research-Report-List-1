<p><ul><li>Mould Lens Edger</li><li>Mould Free Lens Edger</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual optical lens edger market is categorized into two main types: mould lens edgers and mould-free lens edgers. Mould lens edgers are designed to shape lenses using pre-formed molds, ensuring precision and consistency in lens curvature. In contrast, mould-free lens edgers utilize customizable tools that allow for greater flexibility in design, accommodating various lens shapes and prescriptions. Both types cater to different production needs, with mould lens edgers often favored for high-volume operations, while mould-free options suit bespoke requirements.</p></p>
