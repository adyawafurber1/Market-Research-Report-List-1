<p><p>Wire forming machines are categorized into CNC (Computer Numerical Control) and Non-CNC systems. CNC wire forming machines utilize computer control for precise and automated shaping of wire, enhancing efficiency and accuracy for complex designs. They are ideal for high-volume production. In contrast, Non-CNC wire forming machines rely on manual setups and mechanical controls, offering flexibility for smaller batches or simpler designs. While CNC machines are better suited for intricate tasks, Non-CNC machines provide cost-effective solutions for varied applications.</p></p>
