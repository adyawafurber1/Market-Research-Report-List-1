<p><p>The monopolar electrode market consists of two primary types: straight and curved electrodes. Straight electrodes are designed for direct application and provide precise control during surgical procedures. They are ideal for straightforward access to tissues. Curved electrodes, on the other hand, feature a contoured shape, allowing for enhanced maneuverability in complex or confined areas. Both types are essential in electrosurgery, facilitating effective cutting and coagulation while offering different advantages based on the specific surgical requirements and anatomical challenges.</p></p>
