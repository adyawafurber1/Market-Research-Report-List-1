<p><p>High Frequency Welded Straight Seam Steel Pipe is categorized primarily into two welding types: Resistance Welding and Induction Welding. Resistance Welding utilizes electrical resistance to generate heat, allowing edges of steel to fuse together as they are pressed under pressure. Induction Welding, on the other hand, employs electromagnetic induction to create heat in the steel, allowing for faster welding speeds and higher precision. Both methods are essential for producing durable, high-quality steel pipes used in various industrial applications.</p></p>
