<p><strong>The Skin Graft Blades Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-Thickness Skin Grafts</li><li>Split-Thickness Skin Grafts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The skin graft blades market is divided into two primary types: full-thickness skin grafts and split-thickness skin grafts. Full-thickness skin grafts involve the removal of both the epidermis and dermis, providing a durable and aesthetically pleasing result for reconstructive procedures. Conversely, split-thickness skin grafts remove only the epidermis and a portion of the dermis, offering a resilient option for larger wound coverage. Each type serves distinct clinical needs, influencing the choice of grafting technique based on the severity of skin damage and desired outcomes.</p></p>
