<p><p>Membrane filter materials for wastewater treatment consist of various types, each serving distinct functions. Ultrafiltration (UF) membranes effectively remove larger particles and organic matter, operating with low pressure. Microfiltration (MF) membranes filter out suspended solids and bacteria, providing a robust barrier against larger contaminants. Reverse osmosis (RO) membranes utilize high pressure to eliminate dissolved salts and other impurities, producing highly purified water. Together, these membrane technologies enhance wastewater treatment efficiency and enable compliance with regulatory standards.</p></p>
