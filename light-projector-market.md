<p><strong>The Light Projector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed-installation projectors</li><li>Pico projectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light projector market can be categorized into fixed-installation projectors and pico projectors. Fixed-installation projectors are large, robust devices designed for permanent setups in venues like auditoriums, schools, and conference rooms, offering high brightness and resolution for professional presentations. In contrast, pico projectors are compact and portable, ideal for personal use or on-the-go presentations. They provide convenience and versatility, allowing users to project images and videos from smartphones or laptops in various settings without the need for extensive setup.</p></p>
