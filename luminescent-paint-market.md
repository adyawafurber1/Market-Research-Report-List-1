<p><strong>The Luminescent Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluorescent Paint</li><li>Phosphorescent Paint</li><li>Radio Luminescent Paint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The luminescent paint market consists of three primary types: fluorescent, phosphorescent, and radio luminescent paints. Fluorescent paint exhibits bright colors that glow under ultraviolet light, ideal for safety signage and artistic applications. Phosphorescent paint absorbs light and re-emits it slowly, creating a glow in the dark effect for use in decorations and emergency pathways. Radio luminescent paint contains radioactive materials, emitting light continuously, often used in watches and instrument dials, though its use is regulated due to safety concerns.</p></p>
