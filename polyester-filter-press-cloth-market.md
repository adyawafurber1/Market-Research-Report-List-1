<p><p>The Polyester Filter Press Cloth market is segmented into two main types: Monofilament and Multifilament filter press cloths. Monofilament filter press cloths are made from single, continuous fibers, offering high drainage speed and easy cleaning, making them ideal for applications requiring high filtration efficiency. In contrast, Multifilament filter press cloths consist of multiple intertwined fibers, providing greater flexibility and surface area, which enhances cake release and filtration capacity. Both types are widely used in industrial filtration processes, each catering to specific operational needs.</p></p>
