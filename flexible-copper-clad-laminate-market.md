<p><ul><li>Tape Casting</li><li>Sputtering</li><li>Electroplating</li><li>Laminating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flexible Copper-Clad Laminate market encompasses several types of manufacturing processes. Tape casting involves spreading a slurry mixture to form thin sheets, which are then dried and cut into desired shapes. Sputtering utilizes physical vapor deposition to apply copper layers onto a substrate, ensuring uniform thickness. Electroplating entails depositing copper onto a surface through an electrochemical process, enhancing conductivity. Laminating combines multiple layers to create a flexible, durable product, ideal for various applications in electronics and circuit boards.</p></p>
