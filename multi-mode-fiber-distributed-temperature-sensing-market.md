<p><p>Multi-Mode Fiber Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS) technology employs two primary market configurations: single-end and loop configurations. The single-end configuration involves deploying a fiber optic cable with sensors connected at one end, enabling efficient temperature monitoring along the fiber length. In contrast, the loop configuration utilizes a closed-loop setup with the fiber running in a continuous circle, which allows for a more extensive sensing range and redundancy. Both configurations cater to various industrial applications where temperature management is critical.</p></p>
