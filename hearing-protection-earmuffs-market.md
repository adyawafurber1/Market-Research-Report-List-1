<p><p>The hearing protection earmuffs market includes two primary types: standard headband style earmuffs and wrap-around earmuffs. Standard headband style earmuffs feature a fixed headband that securely holds the ear cups over the ears, providing effective noise isolation. In contrast, wrap-around earmuffs are designed to fit snugly around the neck, offering flexibility and comfort. Both styles are essential for safeguarding hearing in various environments, serving industries like construction, manufacturing, and recreational use, where noise levels can be harmful.</p></p>
