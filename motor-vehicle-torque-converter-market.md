<p><ul><li>Single-stage Torque Converter</li><li>Multistage Torque Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motor vehicle torque converter market comprises two main types: single-stage and multistage torque converters. A single-stage torque converter uses one set of turbine and impeller blades to transfer power, providing a simpler design and lower cost, ideal for standard vehicles. In contrast, multistage torque converters feature multiple sets of blades, enhancing efficiency and performance across a broader range of engine speeds, making them suitable for high-performance vehicles and applications requiring better torque multiplication and efficiency.</p></p>
