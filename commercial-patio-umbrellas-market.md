<p><p>The commercial patio umbrellas market features three main types: wall-mounted umbrellas, center pole umbrellas, and offset pole umbrellas. Wall-mounted umbrellas are fixed to a wall, ideal for small spaces, providing shade without occupying ground area. Center pole umbrellas have a central support, offering stability and warmth in traditional settings. Offset pole umbrellas feature a side-mounted pole, allowing for versatile positioning, which is perfect for larger outdoor spaces. These options cater to various aesthetic and functional needs in commercial environments.</p></p>
