<p><p>A laboratory colorimeter is an analytical instrument used to measure the concentration of colored solutions by determining the intensity of color. It operates on the principle of light absorption, where a specific wavelength of light passes through a sample, and the amount of light absorbed is directly proportional to the concentration of the substance in that solution. Colorimeters are widely utilized in various sectors, including environmental testing, food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and biochemistry, owing to their accuracy and reliability.</p><p>The laboratory colorimeter market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.2% during the forecast period.
