<p><ul><li>EPS</li><li>HPS</li><li>EHPS</li><li>MS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive steering systems consist of various types designed to enhance vehicle handling and efficiency. Electric Power Steering (EPS) uses electric motors for assistance, improving fuel efficiency and reducing weight. Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS) relies on hydraulic fluid pressure for steering support. Electric-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS) combines both systems, offering the benefits of electric control with hydraulic effectiveness. Meanwhile, Mechanical Steering (MS) provides a traditional approach, relying purely on mechanical linkages, often found in older vehicles, with no electronic or hydraulic assistance.</p></p>
