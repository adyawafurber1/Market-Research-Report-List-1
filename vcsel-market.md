<p><p>The VCSEL (Vertical-Cavity Surface-Emitting Laser) market is segmented into single-mode and multimode types. Single-mode VCSELs offer a narrow optical output, enabling high precision and long-distance communication, ideal for data centers and telecom applications. In contrast, multimode VCSELs emit wider beams, supporting shorter distances with higher data rates, commonly used in short-range communication networks and optical interconnects. Both types cater to different needs in the fast-evolving market for optical connectivity and data transmission technologies.</p></p>
