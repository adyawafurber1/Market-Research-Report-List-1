<p><p>The hydrogen generation market primarily includes traditional alkaline electrolyzers and proton exchange membrane (PEM) electrolyzers. Traditional alkaline electrolyzers utilize a liquid alkaline electrolyte and are known for their cost-effectiveness and scalability, making them suitable for large-scale hydrogen production. PEM electrolyzers, on the other hand, employ a solid polymer membrane, offering higher efficiency, faster response times, and compact designs, ideal for dynamic applications and smaller-scale operations. Both technologies cater to the growing demand for clean hydrogen in various industries.</p></p>
