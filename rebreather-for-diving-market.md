<p><ul><li>Closed Circuit</li><li>Semi-Closed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diving rebreather market is mainly categorized into two types: closed circuit and semi-closed circuit systems. Closed circuit rebreathers recycle exhaled air, removing carbon dioxide and adding oxygen, allowing for longer dives with minimal bubbles, making them ideal for underwater exploration. In contrast, semi-closed circuit rebreathers also recycle gas but vent some exhaled gas to the environment, providing a balance between efficiency and simplicity. Both systems are used in recreational diving and technical applications, each serving specific diver needs.</p></p>
