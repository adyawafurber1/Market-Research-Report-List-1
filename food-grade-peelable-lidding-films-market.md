<p><p>Food grade peelable lidding films are categorized based on the material used. Polypropylene (PP) is widely recognized for its high clarity and low sealing temperatures. Polyethylene (PE) provides excellent moisture barriers and is flexible. Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) offers superior resistance to chemicals and UV light. Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) is popular for its affordability and versatility, though less environmentally friendly. Polyamide (PA) exhibits strong puncture resistance and is often used in vacuum applications. Other materials include biodegradable options, catering to sustainability trends.</p></p>
